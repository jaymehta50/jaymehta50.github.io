If you had a brain, you'd buy some.

Addenbrooke’s Charity Pantomime proudly presents this year’s show: the Wizard of Obs!

Join us this year in the land of Obs, as medical student Dorothalassemia and her crew fights against the Wicked Witch of Westminster in an attempt to rescue Addenbrookes while the EPIC storm hits. What will happen down the yellow slick road? Will they find all they’re looking for in the mighty Wizard of Obs?

A student production by Cambridge Clinical school of Medicine, with all your favourite medical puns, consultants, and medleys!

Voted 4 stars by Cambridge Tab, ‘an evening of hilarity and entertainment that is not to be missed!’


Not suitable for children due to mature comedy and sexual themes.

Have a heart

This year, we are proud to support the following charities....




As our regular annual charity, ACT are dedicated to supporting innovation in patient care across Cambridge University Hospitals NHS Foundation Trust, which runs Addenbrooke’s and the Rosie hospitals.

This year we want to support Coram Cambridgeshire Adoption, who work to find forever families for children who need permanent, loving homes.

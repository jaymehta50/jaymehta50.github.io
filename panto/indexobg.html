Addenbrooke's Charity Pantomime 2016 proudly presents


Tuesday 26th to Saturday 30th January 2016

Tickets

If you had a brain, you'd buy some.

Addenbrooke's Charity Pantomime 2016 presents Peter PancreasThe Wizard of Obs; a medical themed take on a classic. There will be puns, munchkins and plenty of poor medical students making fools of themselves. Come join us for a cornucopia of singing, dancing, innuendos and cringe worthy jokes.

This year we join Peter Pancreas as he fights against the dastardly Captain Hookworm and his crew of miscreant consultants. Will he succeed or will the villainous pirates triumph? Who knows what will happen in this eccentric and fun take on pantomime! It's produced, written, directed, choreographed, orchestrated and acted by students from the Cambridge Clinical School with cameos from some of our favourite consultants.

Guaranteed to be a fun night whether you are medical or not, last year's panto got 4 stars by the Tab with them reporting "The entire show is packed full of more innuendos than a Carry On film."

This year we will be performing in aid of the Addenbrooke's Charitable Trust and Cambridge Mind, with all profits from the show and interval raffle donated to these worthwhile causes.

Not suitable for those averse to cheesy songs or those with a delicate disposition!


Not suitable for children due to mature comedy and sexual themes.
